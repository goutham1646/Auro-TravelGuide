What Makes This Place Special

Auroville is an experimental township founded in 1968, designed to be a universal city where people from all nations can live in peace and harmony. The most iconic structure here is the Matrimandir, a golden sphere that serves as a meditation center. Auroville represents a unique blend of spirituality, sustainable living, and international community. The township spans over 20 square kilometers and is home to people from more than 50 countries. The lush green surroundings, organic farms, and eco-friendly practices make it a fascinating destination for those interested in alternative living and spiritual exploration.

Entry Details

Free or Paid: Entry to Auroville is free. However, visiting the Matrimandir requires prior registration and is free of charge.
Prior Permission Required: Yes, for Matrimandir viewing. You need to register at the Visitor's Center at least one day in advance. Registration opens at 9:00 AM and closes when slots are full (usually by 10:00 AM). Online registration is also available on the official Auroville website.
Timings:
  • Auroville Visitor's Center: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (daily)
  • Matrimandir Viewing: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (by appointment only)
  • Matrimandir Gardens: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (free access, no appointment needed)
Best Time to Visit: Early morning (8:00 AM to 10:00 AM) or late afternoon (4:00 PM to 5:00 PM) to avoid crowds. The weather is pleasant from November to February. Avoid visiting during peak summer months (April to June) due to extreme heat.

What Makes This Place Special

Serenity Beach is one of Puducherry's hidden gems, located about 4 km from Auroville. As the name suggests, this beach offers a peaceful and tranquil atmosphere, making it perfect for those seeking a quiet escape from the more crowded beaches. The beach is relatively clean, with golden sand and clear waters. It's less commercialized compared to other beaches in the area, offering visitors a chance to enjoy nature without the hustle and bustle. The beach is ideal for long walks, meditation, and simply relaxing by the sea. During sunrise and sunset, the beach becomes particularly beautiful, making it a favorite spot for photographers and nature lovers.

Entry Details

Free or Paid: Free entry. No entry fee required.
Prior Permission Required: No permission required. Open to public.
Timings: Open 24 hours, but it's recommended to visit during daylight hours (6:00 AM to 7:00 PM) for safety reasons.
Best Time to Visit: Early morning (6:00 AM to 9:00 AM) for sunrise or late afternoon (4:00 PM to 7:00 PM) for sunset. The beach is most pleasant during the winter months (November to February). Avoid visiting during monsoon season (October to December) when the sea can be rough.

What Makes This Place Special

Auroville Beach, also known as Auro Beach, is a beautiful stretch of coastline located about 3 km from the Auroville Visitor's Center. This beach is popular among both locals and tourists for its clean sands and relatively calm waters. The beach offers a good balance between natural beauty and basic amenities. It's a great spot for swimming, beach games, and relaxation. The beach is well-maintained and offers a more accessible alternative to Serenity Beach while still being less crowded than the main Promenade Beach in Puducherry town. The area around the beach has seen some development with restaurants and basic facilities, making it convenient for visitors while still maintaining its natural charm.

Entry Details

Free or Paid: Free entry. No entry fee required.
Prior Permission Required: No permission required. Open to public.
Timings: Open 24 hours, but recommended visiting during daylight hours (6:00 AM to 7:00 PM) for safety and better experience.
Best Time to Visit: Early morning (6:00 AM to 9:00 AM) for peaceful walks and sunrise, or late afternoon (4:00 PM to 7:00 PM) for sunset views. The beach is most enjoyable during the winter months (November to February). Avoid the peak summer heat (April to June) during midday hours.
